From Mars to Sirius (2005)

Limited Orange Press

Year : 2013

Limited to : 500 copies

Particularities : Signed by the band at "Zicophonies de Clermont" on May 2015

Rarity : 7.5/10

History :

It's with this vinyl that my collection started, before that I just had the albums on CD and the live DVD.
I bought this vinyl at the "Zicophonies de Clermont" on May 22nd 2015 and it's after this concert that I decided to really start my Gojira collection.
The vinyl was for sale on the merchandising stand for 40€, it was already signed.
At the end of this concert I was able to meet Mario with whom I took a picture and he also signed my concert ticket.
